# Color and Shape codes in Little AI

Colors and shapes in Little AI are coded using integer.

*You might ask if there is any logic in the following codes. Haem... no, there's not. Sorry? :innocent:*

## Color codes

__WHITE__: 1

__GREEN__: 2

__RED__: 3

__BLUE__: 4

__ORANGE__: 5

## Shape codes

__CIRCLE__: 1

__SQUARE__: 2

__TRIANGLE__: 3
